Key Features

  • Barcode scanning: Scan packaged foods and add them instantly.
  • Personalized goals: Set custom calorie and macro goals with adaptive support based on progress.
  • Macro-focused home widgets: Check macro progress at a glance with visual indicators.
  • Advanced reports and analytics: Review daily and weekly trends, macro balance, and meal patterns.
  • Water tracking: Log hydration and get context-aware support.
  • Recovery and habit insights: Understand how routines and nutrition patterns evolve over time.
  • Streak and challenge system: Stay motivated with consistency and habit streak tracking.
  • Multilingual support: Turkish, English, German, French, and Spanish.
  • Account and sync: Sign in securely and sync your data across sessions.
