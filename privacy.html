Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

This policy explains what data is processed in CalorieLog, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

  • Nutrition logs, including meals and estimated nutrition values (calories, protein, carbs, fat).
  • Hydration and habit tracking data you enter in the app.
  • Favorites, recent foods, and saved preferences to improve logging speed.
  • Photo-based analysis inputs when you use image recognition features.
  • Barcode scan inputs when you use packaged food lookup.
  • Account and sign-in data (for example, Apple Sign In identifier and email if shared).
  • Subscription metadata (plan status, purchase/renewal/restore events) processed by Apple.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide core functionality: meal logging, macro tracking, hydration tracking, and reports.
  • To power AI features such as nutrition estimation and meal analysis.
  • To sync data across sessions when you use an account.
  • To manage free/pro access and feature limits.
  • To improve reliability, security, and performance.

3. Storage and Infrastructure

Data may be stored locally on your device and/or in cloud infrastructure (including Firebase services such as Authentication and database tools) when account sync is enabled.

4. Third-Party Services

CalorieLog may use third-party providers to operate app features, including Firebase (authentication/sync), Sign in with Apple (account login), and AI processing services used to return meal nutrition estimates. Apple manages billing and subscription payments through your Apple ID account.

5. Subscription and Billing Data

Subscriptions are auto-renewable and processed by Apple. We do not store your full payment card details. Apple may share limited subscription status information needed to grant Pro access and restore purchases.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied for data security. However, absolute security cannot be guaranteed for internet transmission or cloud systems.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You can update or delete many entries directly in the app. You can also request account/data support via the contact email below.

8. Children's Privacy

CalorieLog is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Medical Disclaimer

CalorieLog is a wellness tracking app and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version is indicated by the "Last Updated" date above.

11. Contact
